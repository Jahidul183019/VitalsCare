WHO Diabetes Screening & Care Guidelines (2024)
- Screening for diabetes should be performed using fasting blood glucose or HbA1c tests.
- Adults with a BMI over 25 kg/m2 or family history should be screened periodically.
- Lifestyle advice should focus on nutrition: reduce simple carbohydrates, eliminate sugar-sweetened beverages.
- Encourage eating high-fiber foods such as whole grains, vegetables, and lentils.
- Recommend physical activity like brisk walking or cycling for 30 minutes daily.
- Early screening helps prevent microvascular complications (neuropathy, retinopathy, nephropathy).
- Maintain target HbA1c below 7.0% for most non-pregnant adults.
