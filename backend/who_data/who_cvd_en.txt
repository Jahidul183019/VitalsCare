WHO Cardiovascular Disease (CVD) Prevention Guidelines (2024)
- Total cardiovascular risk assessment should incorporate age, BP, smoking status, BMI, and family history.
- Tobacco cessation is the most effective intervention for reducing cardiovascular event risks.
- Advise a heart-healthy diet low in saturated fats and trans-fats, and rich in whole grains.
- Recommend active stress management and adequate sleep (7-8 hours per night).
- Physical exercise improves cardiorespiratory fitness and lowers blood pressure.
- High-risk patients should be evaluated for aspirin, statin, or beta-blocker therapy by a physician.
