WHO Hypertension Guidelines (2024)
- Diagnosis of hypertension should be confirmed by a clinical professional.
- Adults with hypertension should have their blood pressure measured at least annually.
- Lifestyle modifications include reducing sodium intake to less than 2g/day (approx 5g of salt).
- Promote physical activity of at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic exercise per week.
- Limit alcohol consumption and completely avoid tobacco smoking.
- Patients with systolic blood pressure >= 140 mmHg or diastolic >= 90 mmHg require regular clinic reviews.
- For high-risk individuals, drug treatment should be initiated immediately alongside lifestyle therapy.
